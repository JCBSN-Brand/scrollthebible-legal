Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2024

Scroll The Bible ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Favorite verses you save
  • Notes you create
  • Reading preferences and settings
  • Subscription status

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

AI Bible Study Feature

When you use our AI-powered Bible study features, the verse text you're studying is sent to OpenAI's API to generate explanations and insights. We do not store these queries or responses on our servers. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle data.

Subscription Information

Subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy practices.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Browsing history outside the app
  • Contacts or photos

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. We do not maintain external servers that store your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
